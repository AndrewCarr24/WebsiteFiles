Building a Recommendation System with Beer Data

Beer culture in the United States has changed dramatically in the past decade or so. This trend is reflected in the development of a vibrant community of people who rate, review, and share information about beers online. Websites like BeerAdvocate, RateBeer, and Untappd give beer drinkers a place to share their beer tastes with others.
